About me

I'm a Vietnamese student currently pursing my Master degree titled Information Engineering and Computer Sciences at Rhein-Waal University of Applied Sciences in Kamp-Lintfort, Germany. Previously, I completed my Bachelor programme in Communication and Information Engineering at the same university.

For my bachelor thesis, I worked under the student-initiated research project "Phenix" which aims to build a smart UAV that is capable of avoid collision through Deep Neural Network for applications in indoor Search and Rescue operations.

Besides studying, I am also working part-time at FabLab Kamp-Lintfort, one of many digital fabrication labs around the world. Here, my task is to support students with digital fabrication assignments ranging from 2D designing to building electronics.

I also work at RF-Frontend GmbH, a company providing engineering solutions relating to antenna measurement and more industrial applications. Here, my main task is to develop and maintain softwares which keep track of company's inventory or perform automated measurements.
